<div class="jumbotron-tagline">This is home now, the red-dirt edge of nothing.</div>

300 years in the future, Earth is a playground for the rich and a hellscape for the poor. Political corruption and corporate power have led to the establishment of the Penitentiary Colonies of Mars, where Earth's convicts are sent to permanent exile, and where the descendants of the first prisoners still share that same fate.

Trapped at a 19th-century tech level by their prison wardens, the mysterious NILE corporation, the inhabitants of the town of Virtue make their way as best they can: wrangling cattle and fighting bears and bandits under the forever unreachable stars.

Welcome to Mars. Here's your spurs.
